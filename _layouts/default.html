Tahsin Hossain

I am a 3rd year mechanical engineering student at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), Khulna, Bangladesh. My primary focus is on computational combustion. I'm interested in premixed flames, turbulence-chemistry interaction, and high-performance computing.

I have prevoiusly worked with Fahim Islam Anik to review the application of neural networks for combustion flow-field reconstruction, and currently working with Dr. Md. Ashraful Islam on the application of Functionally Graded Materials (FGM) for shocktube applications.
